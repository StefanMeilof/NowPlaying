Questions
Do I need to create an account?
Sort of, but it takes 10 seconds. Enter your email, click the magic link we send you, pick a username. That's the entire setup. No password, ever.
How do I add videos?
Three ways. Paste a YouTube link into the chat dashboard on your page. On Android, hit share on any YouTube video and pick NowPlaying from the list. Or send a link via Telegram if you prefer.
What happens after 10 days?
The video disappears from your page automatically. You don't have to do anything. Add a new link any time to keep your page alive.
Can people see my email?
No. Your public page only shows your username and the videos you share. Your email is never visible to anyone.
Does it work with Spotify or Apple Music?
YouTube links only for now. Most tracks, live sets, and music videos are on YouTube anyway, so you're rarely stuck.
Is it free?
Yes. Free to use, no subscription, no ads.
